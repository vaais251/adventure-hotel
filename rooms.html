Booking Policies

Check-in/Check-out

  • Check-in time: 2:00 PM
  • Check-out time: 11:00 AM
  • Early check-in and late check-out available upon request and subject to availability

Cancellation Policy

  • Free cancellation up to 7 days before arrival
  • Cancellations made 3-7 days before arrival: 50% of the total booking amount will be charged
  • Cancellations made less than 3 days before arrival: 100% of the total booking amount will be charged

Children and Extra Beds

  • Children under 5 years stay free when using existing bedding
  • Extra beds are available for an additional charge of 1000 PKR per night

Payment

  • A 30% deposit is required to confirm your reservation
  • Full payment can be made upon arrival
  • We accept cash (PKR) and major credit cards
