Our Story

The Adventure Saria Story

Adventure Saria was born from a deep love for the majestic Gilgit-Baltistan region and a desire to share its breathtaking beauty with travelers from around the world. Founded by a family with generations of roots in the area, our hotel combines local traditions with modern comfort.

The name "Saria" comes from the local Balti language, meaning "a place of rest and gathering." For centuries, the people of Gilgit-Baltistan have welcomed travelers with legendary hospitality, offering shelter and warmth amid the towering mountains.

We opened our doors in 2018 with a simple mission: to create a haven where guests can experience the authentic culture, cuisine, and natural wonders of this remarkable region while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed hotel.

Today, Adventure Saria stands as a gateway to the wonders of Gilgit-Baltistan, from the mighty peaks of the Karakoram Range to the lush valleys and glacial lakes that make this region so special. We are proud to employ local staff who bring genuine Baltistani hospitality to every interaction.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide our guests with an authentic Baltistani experience, combining comfort, adventure, and cultural immersion while preserving and celebrating the natural beauty and traditions of Gilgit-Baltistan.

Our Values

  • Authentic Hospitality: We embrace the centuries-old tradition of Baltistani hospitality, treating each guest as family.
  • Environmental Responsibility: We are committed to sustainable practices that protect the pristine natural environment of Gilgit-Baltistan.
  • Cultural Preservation: We actively promote and preserve local culture, arts, and traditions through our activities and experiences.
  • Community Support: We prioritize local employment and sourcing, ensuring that tourism benefits the community.
  • Adventure with Safety: We provide thrilling experiences while maintaining the highest safety standards for our guests.
