Getting Here

There are several ways to reach Adventure Saria Hotel from major cities in Pakistan:

By Air

Regular flights operate from Islamabad to Gilgit Airport. The hotel is approximately 25 minutes drive from the airport.

Note: Flight schedules can vary based on weather conditions in the mountains.

By Bus

Comfortable bus services connect Islamabad to Gilgit. The journey takes approximately 12-15 hours along the scenic Karakoram Highway.

NATCO and other private companies operate luxury buses with overnight services.

By Car

For those who enjoy driving, the Karakoram Highway offers one of the world's most spectacular road trips from Islamabad to Gilgit-Baltistan.

We recommend experienced drivers only and to allow 2 days with an overnight stay en route.

Hotel Transfers

We offer private transfer services from Gilgit Airport or Bus Station directly to Adventure Saria Hotel.

Contact our reservation team to arrange a pickup when booking your stay.
