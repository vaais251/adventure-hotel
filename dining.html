Our Culinary Experience

At Adventure Saria, dining is not just about nourishment; it's an integral part of your cultural journey through Gilgit-Baltistan. Our restaurant offers a perfect blend of authentic local cuisine and international flavors, prepared with locally-sourced, fresh ingredients that celebrate the rich agricultural heritage of the region.

Mountain View Restaurant

Perched on the hotel's top floor, our restaurant offers panoramic views of the majestic Karakoram Range. Our culinary team, led by Chef Ahmad, brings together traditional recipes passed down through generations with contemporary cooking techniques.

Whether you're starting your day with a hearty breakfast before an adventure, enjoying a light lunch, or savoring a leisurely dinner while watching the sunset paint the mountains in golden hues, our restaurant provides the perfect backdrop for a memorable dining experience.

Opening Hours:

  • Breakfast: 7:00 AM - 10:30 AM
  • Lunch: 12:30 PM - 3:00 PM
  • Dinner: 7:00 PM - 10:30 PM

Reservations: While hotel guests have priority seating, we welcome outside visitors. Reservations are recommended, especially for dinner service.
