Trekking Expeditions

Experience the Magic of Gilgit-Baltistan on Foot

Our trekking expeditions take you through some of the most spectacular landscapes on earth. From day hikes to multi-day treks, we offer adventures suitable for all experience levels.

Popular Trekking Routes:

  • Fairy Meadows and Nanga Parbat Base Camp (3-4 days)
  • Rakaposhi Base Camp Trek (2-3 days)
  • Naltar Valley Trek (1-2 days)
  • Shimshal Pass Trek (5-7 days)
  • Hunza Valley Cultural Trek (1-3 days)

What's Included:

  • Professional local guide
  • Camping equipment (for multi-day treks)
  • Meals during the trek
  • Porter assistance for luggage
  • First aid kit and safety equipment

What to Bring:

  • Sturdy hiking boots
  • Weather-appropriate clothing (layers recommended)
  • Personal medications
  • Camera
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses

Mountain Climbing

Conquer the Mighty Peaks of the Karakoram

The Karakoram Range offers some of the world's most challenging and rewarding mountain climbing experiences. Our expert guides will lead you safely through these epic adventures.

Climbing Expeditions:

  • Spantik Peak (7,027m) - Technical Climb (10-14 days)
  • Diran Peak (7,257m) - Challenging Expedition (12-16 days)
  • Laila Peak (6,096m) - Iconic Ski Mountaineering (8-12 days)
  • Rush Peak (5,098m) - Beginner Friendly Expedition (5-7 days)

Requirements:

Mountain climbing requires previous experience and physical fitness. Our team will assess your skill level and recommend appropriate expeditions. We offer preparatory training for those who need it.

What's Included:

  • Professional climbing guides
  • Technical climbing equipment
  • High-altitude camps and equipment
  • Meals during the expedition
  • Permits and administrative support

What's Not Included:

  • Personal climbing gear
  • International travel insurance
  • Personal expenses

White Water Rafting

Navigate the Thrilling Rapids of the Indus River

Experience the adrenaline rush of white water rafting on the mighty Indus River, one of the most legendary rivers in Asia. Our rafting adventures offer a unique perspective of the stunning gorges and valleys.

Rafting Options:

  • Beginners' Course: Class I-II Rapids (2 hours)
  • Half-Day Adventure: Class II-III Rapids (4 hours)
  • Full-Day Experience: Class III-IV Rapids with lunch (7 hours)
  • Multi-Day Expedition: Full river journey with camping (2-3 days)

What's Included:

  • Professional river guides
  • All rafting equipment (boats, paddles, helmets)
  • Safety gear and life jackets
  • Refreshments (water, snacks)
  • Meals (for full-day and multi-day options)
  • Transportation to and from the river

Note: No previous rafting experience is necessary for beginners' and half-day adventures. Children above 12 years are welcome on the beginner course.

Mountain Biking

Explore on Two Wheels

Discover the breathtaking landscapes of Gilgit-Baltistan on mountain bikes. Our guided biking tours take you through scenic trails, local villages, and spectacular mountain views.

Biking Routes:

  • Hunza Valley Circuit (Full day, 25-30km)
  • Naltar Forest Trail (Half day, 15km)
  • Karimabad - Altit Loop (Half day, 10-12km)
  • Shigar Valley Exploration (Full day, 20-25km)

What's Included:

  • High-quality mountain bikes
  • Helmets and safety gear
  • Local guide familiar with all trails
  • Support vehicle (for full-day trips)
  • Refreshments and lunch (for full-day trips)

Suitable for intermediate riders with basic mountain biking experience. Custom routes can be arranged for beginners or advanced riders upon request.

Cultural Tours

Immerse in Rich Local Traditions

The cultural landscape of Gilgit-Baltistan is as diverse and captivating as its natural beauty. Our cultural tours provide authentic experiences and meaningful interactions with local communities.

Cultural Experiences:

  • Ancient Forts Tour (Altit & Baltit Forts)
  • Traditional Crafts Workshops (carpet weaving, embroidery)
  • Village Homestay Experience
  • Local Cuisine Cooking Class
  • Festival Celebrations (seasonal)

What's Included:

  • Knowledgeable cultural guide
  • Site entrance fees
  • Traditional meals
  • Workshop materials (where applicable)
  • Transportation between sites

These tours provide economic support directly to local communities and help preserve traditional practices and heritage.

Photography Tours

Capture the Beauty of the Karakoram

Our specialized photography tours are designed to help you capture the magnificent landscapes, unique cultural moments, and spectacular light conditions that make Gilgit-Baltistan a photographer's paradise.

Photography Experiences:

  • Sunset/Sunrise Mountain Photography (Rakaposhi, Ultar, Hunza)
  • Cultural Portrait Photography in Traditional Villages
  • Night Sky & Star Trail Photography
  • Wildlife Photography Excursions
  • Seasonal Photography (Autumn colors, Spring blossoms)

What's Included:

  • Photography guide who knows the best locations and lighting
  • Transportation to photography spots
  • Permits for special access areas
  • Refreshments during the tour

Bring your own camera equipment. Our guides can provide advice on settings and composition but specialized photography training is not included.
